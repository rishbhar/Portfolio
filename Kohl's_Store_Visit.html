Small Research - Kohl's iPhone application, and store visit


`
E-commerce on mobile is definitely a different experience than on a website. It is more personal, and the communication between the customer and the product is seamless and more context based. A simple feature like reminding the customer about Kohl's when he/she is passing by a store, or when a customer is near a store's location, adds to the whole experience. I downloaded the mobile application and started using it. When I got to know that Kohl's mobile application has additional features that can be used at a store, I decided to visit the nearest one. I had never been to a Kohl's store. After doing some secondary research, I found out that Kohl's have kiosks set up in their stores. From where I come, I had never used a dedicated kiosk which was provided by the store. So, I wanted to use that system and understand how it would enhance my experience.

Kohl's mobile application

I signed up on the application. The first screen showed me options to look for different kinds of products for men, women and children. Besides that, there is a section for discount offers and Today's Deals. This is a good starting point because a new user would choose any one of these sections to start browsing.

There is a text field on the top for a user to directly search for any product available to browse. On the right side of the field there is a bar code scanner, which made me think how I can use it if I am a new user.

Search bar, barcode scanner and different sections for browsing


THOUGHTS
1) I could use the bar code if I want to look up a product in the app when I am at the store.
2) Why would I use the bar code if I am at home, and I am looking up different products in the app?
3) If a customer does not want to do free shipping, 'BUY ONLINE, PICK UP IN STORE' is pretty cool.
4) If I open the 'MEN' category from the menu, the app takes me to the Men's products screen and I can browse. Now because the search bar is available I thought that I will be searching only in the Men's products, but it is not like that. I can again search for anything. I am not sure how this affects a user's experience, but it does not follow hierarchy.
5) When browsing the app I can change the view settings, which is a good feature. Users can change that as per their preferences.










6) I can read reviews on a product, and can even sort reviews based on different criteria. This is very useful because customers have different preferences, and reviews are one of strongest motivators for buying a product, or proof of quality for a product.






7) Having the option to check the availability of a product at the nearest store, and buying it for pick up is cool! It saves me time and effort. This really speaks to what Kohl's values, Empowering customers!


Store visit

The application has a lot more to explore, but it was time to visit the store and see if I could use the application to better my experience at the store. I was excited!! On my way, I got a notification when I was near the store's location. It prompted me to use the store tools.



Reaching the store. Part of my experience.



Thanks to my friends who drove me there. Kohl's logo on the wall looked great!



Up on the wall at the entrance, there are pictures of kids enjoying, having fun in the park. The beginning of the at-the-store experience.


I found the kiosk! Now it was time to poke fingers into the screen.



A nice long screen. I also got curious about the panel below the screen



Panel with options to call for assistance, scan barcode, pay and print out something



I could choose different sizes and colors. It was great that I could locate it in the store too!


About the touch interface, at times I had to press twice or thrice to interact with the screen. Actually I realized, tapping on the screen worked better than just pressing harder on the screen.



Isn't it awesome!? I was not expecting anything like this, loved it



Information on other stores if the product is not available in the store you are at.



Print outs for store locations and directions


While I was learning how to use the kiosk, I checked the app.



Launching store tools! 1, 2, 3..



It was nice that I could just scan the barcode and pull up all the information on a product.



I scanned the bar code for this sweater.



Now I could check what all colors they have in this sweater on the app only


Features like the Wallet, Registry and Lists are also available, which I did not get the time to explore. It was fun going to the store. I also talked to one of their staff members, who helped me in understanding how to use the kiosk's panel.



Thanks to Kim for taking some time out and talking to me.


What I learned about Kohl's mobile app and store

1) Kohl's mobile app gives the user a lot to explore. Not only just buying from the app, but also a lot many in-store features, which is great!
2) The mobile application can use some improvement on the usage of product listing hierarchy in the menu.
3) Using multiple filters was not a very smooth experience. I had to switch many screens.
4) The kiosk at the store had useful features like locating the product and browing similar products, which saves customer's time and effort.
All comments are based on the limited experience I had with the application and the store kiosks.
