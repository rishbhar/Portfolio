ABOUT ME

I come from a Computer Science background with around 3 years of work experience, where I have worked on multiple projects with clients from different parts of India. I got introduced to Human Computer Interaction while working on an independent project. Since then, I had developed an interest in this field. Before starting my Masters in HCI-Design at IU, I interned as a UX Designer at a startup.

Studying HCI Design has given me the opportunity to leverage my skills as a team player, and learn how to find a solution to a problem considering various stakeholders. I am currently looking for an internship opportunity for Summer 2016.

I am a music and movies enthusiast, and I like to play guitar to relax, revel, and sometimes to rejuvenate myself.
